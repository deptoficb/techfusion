Why Join TechFusion?

Get ready for TechFusion 2025, a 3-day Inter-College hackathon happening from October 13–15, 2025! Join brilliant minds from across colleges to innovate, code, and create the future. With a ₹1,00,000 prize pool and challenges spread across 6 exciting domains—IoT, Data Science, Cybersecurity, AI/ML, Web Development, and Open Innovation—this is your chance to showcase your skills, collaborate with peers, and build solutions that matter.

Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, TechFusion 2025 is the perfect platform to learn, network, and compete. Don’t just watch the future happen—create it with us!
